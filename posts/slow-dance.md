---
title: ▍Slow Dance
created: 2026-07-17
modified: 2026-07-17
authors: David L. Weatherford
category: Poem
tags: []
---

> Have you ever watched kids on a merry-go-round?
>
> Or listened to the rain slapping on the ground?
>
> Ever followed a butterfly’s erratic flight?
>
> Or gazed at the sun into the fading night?
>
> <mark>You better slow down.</mark>
>
> <mark>Don’t dance so fast.</mark>
>
> <mark>Time is short.</mark>
>
> <mark>The music won’t last.</mark>
>
> Do you run through each day on the fly?
>
> When you ask: “How are you?” do you hear the reply?
>
> When the day is done, do you lie in your bed, with the next hundred chores running through your head?
>
> <mark>You’d better slow down.</mark>
>
> <mark>Don’t dance so fast.</mark>
>
> <mark>Time is short.</mark>
>
> <mark>The music won’t last.</mark>
>
> Ever told your child, we’ll do it tomorrow?
>
> And in your haste, not see his sorrow?
>
> Ever lost touch, let a good friendship die, cause you never had time to call and say “Hi”?
>
> <mark>You’d better slow down.</mark>
>
> <mark>Don’t dance so fast.</mark>
>
> <mark>Time is short.</mark>
>
> <mark>The music won’t last.</mark>
>
> When you run so fast to get somewhere, you miss half the fun of getting there.
>
> When you worry and hurry through your day, it is like an unopened gift thrown away.
>
> <mark>Life is not a race.</mark>
>
> <mark>Do take it slower.</mark>
>
> <mark>Hear the music before the song is over.</mark>

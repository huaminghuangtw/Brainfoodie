# 1. Do hard things

Understand that life-improving changes almost always get “worse first” before they get better.

> Everything you want is on the other side of worse first.

# 2. Do your thing

**Choose Your Suffering:** There is no completely safe, struggle-free path in life. Since you are going to face challenges _either way,_ choose a path that is worth suffering for.

> “Life is suffering, so pick something worth suffering for.”

# 3. Do it for decades

If you stay excited about one specific pursuit for a decade or longer, there is no obstacle that won’t eventually yield to your accumulated growth.

# 4. Write your story

**Ignore the “How” Initially:** Do not let the logistics or fear of _how_ you will achieve a dream kill the dream itself. Draft a bold vision of your life five years from now, put blinders on to align your daily actions, and let the execution fall into place.

> The “How” is the killer of all great dreams.
